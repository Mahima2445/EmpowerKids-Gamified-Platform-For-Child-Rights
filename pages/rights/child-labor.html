Child Labor Rights

Learn About Child Labor Protection

Child labor protection rights ensure children are safe from harmful work:

  • Right to education instead of work
  • Protection from dangerous jobs
  • Right to play and rest
  • Protection from exploitation
  • Safe working conditions when appropriate

Why protection from child labor matters:

  • Children deserve to learn and grow
  • Work can be harmful to development
  • Education opens future opportunities
  • Children should have time to play
  • Fair treatment protects their health
