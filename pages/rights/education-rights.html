What are Education Rights?

Education rights ensure that every child has access to quality education that develops their talents and abilities.

Free Primary Education

Education should be free and accessible to all children

Equal Access

All children have equal opportunities regardless of background

Safe Learning Environment

Schools should provide a safe and supportive atmosphere
