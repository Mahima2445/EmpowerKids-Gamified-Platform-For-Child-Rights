Education Rights Overview

Right to Education (RTE) Act

The RTE Act is a landmark legislation in India that ensures free and compulsory education for all children aged 6 to 14 years. It guarantees every child the right to quality education, regardless of their background, and holds schools accountable for providing a safe and inclusive learning environment.

Free Education

No fees for children aged 6-14 in government schools

25% Reservation

Private schools must reserve seats for disadvantaged children

Quality Standards

Schools must meet infrastructure and teaching quality requirements
